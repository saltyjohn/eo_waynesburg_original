R.I.P.

The Great Big Keg in the Sky Club

----------

  

Mark "Rat" Lewis ('70), Brother & Alumni Chapter President 

 

Mark and I were not only fraternity brothers in the Burg, we were friends. We first starting planning reunions back in 2001 along with other brothers. He had a real passion to make each reunion the best reunion ever. We developed a special bond   which grew over the years. When we got together, wherever it was, he always wanted to call other brothers on the phone to connect, catch up and  talk about the next reunion. When he traveled out to Pittsburgh, he always wanted to gather a group of brothers and Waynesburg friends for dinner. These dinners became a tradition. This was Mark, always  wanting to gather a group of old friends and stay connected.  

As you all know, Mark served as our Alumni President for many years and he really was passionate about his role. He will be missed but certainly never forgotten. May he rest in peace. This coming reunion will be bittersweet without his presence and it is important, for all who can, to attend this reunion as we need to appreciate our special moments together. Our brotherhood is like a family and we have just lost one of our special brothers. 

Ben Lyster/"Benute"
